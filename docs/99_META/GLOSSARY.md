> *"Shared language reduces hidden complexity."*

# Why This Document Exists

This glossary defines recurring terms used throughout the AI Architect Knowledge Platform.

It exists to keep product, architecture, writing, and AI-assisted development aligned around the same vocabulary.

---

# Terms

## AI Architect Knowledge Platform

The complete professional knowledge system represented by this repository, its documentation, and the public website interface.

## Professional Knowledge Operating System

The broader concept that this repository is not only a portfolio, but a structured system for capturing engineering judgment, product thinking, architecture, and evidence.

## Single Source of Truth

The principle that every important concept should have one canonical explanation, with other documents referencing it rather than duplicating it.

## Decision Framework

The canonical product and architecture decision-making model defined in `docs/00_FOUNDATION/04_DECISION_FRAMEWORK.md`.

## Living Document

A document that represents current understanding and should evolve when evidence improves or contradicts that understanding.

## Evidence

Concrete proof of engineering judgment, such as case studies, decision records, production lessons, open-source work, architecture notes, and measurable outcomes.

---

## Living Document

Engineering evolves continuously.

This document captures current principles rather than immutable truths.

When new evidence contradicts an existing belief, the document should be updated accordingly.

Strong engineering values learning over consistency.
